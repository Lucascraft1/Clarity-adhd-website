We're cash-pay only — which means no prior authorizations, no coverage denials, and no surprise bills. We provide itemized superbills you can submit to your insurer for potential out-of-network reimbursement. HSA and FSA cards are accepted, and flexible payment plans are available.

We serve all of Washington State via secure telehealth video. You must be physically located in Washington at the time of your appointment.

The QBTest is an FDA-cleared, computerized test that objectively measures attention, impulsivity, and activity level. Unlike symptom checklists alone, it gives us real data to guide treatment decisions and track your progress over time.

Three systematic trials cover the vast majority of presentations. If we haven't found the right fit by Week 6, we'll discuss next steps together — including additional medication options, adjunct strategies, or referrals when appropriate.

Most primary care ADHD appointments are 15 minutes and result in a single prescription. Our program includes a 60-minute evaluation, objective QBTest data, three systematic medication trials, and a long-term maintenance plan.
