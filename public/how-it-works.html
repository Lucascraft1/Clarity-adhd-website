Your 6-Week Program

How It Works

A structured, data-driven path to the treatment that actually works for your brain.

Most ADHD care starts with a short appointment and the first medication on the list. If it doesn't work, you wait, reschedule, and try again, sometimes for months. Clarity ADHD runs the process the way it should be run: a structured six-week program that combines a thorough evaluation, objective testing, and systematic medication trials, so decisions are driven by data instead of guesswork.

Week by Week

The program, step by step

Week 1

Comprehensive Evaluation

A 60-minute deep-dive into your history, symptoms, and goals. We review childhood and adult patterns, prior diagnoses and medications, and co-occurring concerns like depression and anxiety. No 15-minute intake, no checklist-only screening.

Week 2

QbTest + First Medication Trial

You complete the FDA-cleared QbTest, which objectively measures attention, impulsivity, and motor activity. Combined with your evaluation, this data establishes your baseline, and your first medication trial begins.

Weeks 3–4

Medication Trials #2 and #3

We systematically trial and compare up to three medication options with weekly check-in visits. Instead of settling for "good enough," we gather real evidence about how each option works for your brain.

Week 6

Optimization & Long-Term Plan

We review everything together: your experience, side effects, and the data. Then we choose the best fit and establish your long-term maintenance plan.

After Week 6

What happens next

Once your treatment is optimized, ongoing care is simple: maintenance visits for medication management and refills, with extended visits available when something more complex comes up.
