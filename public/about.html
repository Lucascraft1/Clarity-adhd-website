Why ADHD

Why I Specialize in Adult ADHD Care

Lucas's interest in ADHD started before his career did. Diagnosed with ADHD himself, he spent years navigating the same frustrations his patients describe: being misunderstood, dismissed, or treated with a one-size-fits-all approach that never quite fit.

Watching the difference an accurate diagnosis and thoughtful treatment made, for himself and for the patients he's since worked with, is what drove him to specialize in adult ADHD care. That perspective, having lived it and having treated it, shapes how he approaches every evaluation: carefully, systematically, and without assuming a patient's experience is exaggerated or invented.

Clinical Philosophy

My Approach to Care

Adult ADHD is frequently missed, misdiagnosed, or treated with a rushed, one-size-fits-all approach. Lucas built Clarity ADHD around a more careful, evidence-based process, one that treats every evaluation as its own investigation rather than a formality on the way to a prescription.

Objective Testing

FDA-cleared QbTest measures attention, impulsivity, and activity objectively. Data guides treatment decisions, not guesswork.

Systematic Medication Trials

Three systematic medication trials, compared head-to-head, to find what actually works for each patient's brain.

Unhurried Evaluations

A 60-minute initial evaluation. Complex presentations deserve careful, unrushed attention.

Continuity of Care

Patients see the same provider at every visit. No rotating clinicians, no re-explaining your history.
